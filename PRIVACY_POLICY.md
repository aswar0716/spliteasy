# Privacy Policy — SplitEasy

**Last updated: March 2026**

## What SplitEasy collects

SplitEasy stores all data **locally on your device only**:
- Friends list and group names
- Bill history and split results
- Journal entries

**No data is sent to our servers.** We have no servers.

## API Keys you provide

SplitEasy lets you optionally enter third-party API keys:
- **OpenAI API key** — used only to send receipt images to OpenAI for parsing. Stored locally on your device. Never sent to us.
- **Splitwise API key** — used only to post expenses to your Splitwise account. Stored locally on your device. Never sent to us.

Receipt images are sent directly from your device to OpenAI's servers for processing and are not stored by SplitEasy.

## Camera and Photos

SplitEasy requests access to your camera and photo library solely to photograph or select receipt images for AI parsing. Images are not stored by the app beyond the current session.

## Third-party services

- **OpenAI** — receipt image parsing (optional). Subject to OpenAI's privacy policy.
- **Splitwise** — expense syncing (optional). Subject to Splitwise's privacy policy.
